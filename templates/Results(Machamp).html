Machamp are said to know every martial arts style in the world. It punches extremely fast, throwing five hundred punches a second. With only one hand, it can move a mountain. The four arms react much faster than Machamp can think. However, it has poor dexterity, and cannot handle delicate work without its arms becoming tangled.
